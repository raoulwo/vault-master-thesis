# Quality of Service (QoS)

*Quality of Service (QoS)* is the description or measurement of the overall performance of a service (computer network or cloud computing service). Several aspects are considered:

- [[Packet Loss]]
- [[Bitrate]]
- [[Throughput]]
- [[Transmission Delay]]
- [[Availability]]
- [[Jitter]]

In the field of *networks*, QoS has a slightly different meaning: Rather than describing the user perceived service quality, it describes the traffic prioritization and resource reservation control mechanisms of the (packet-switched) network. QoS enables providing different priorities to different applications or users.
