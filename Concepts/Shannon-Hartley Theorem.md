# Shannon-Hartley Theorem

Describes the *maximum rate at which information can be transmitted over a communications channel* of specified bandwidth in the presence of noise:

$$C = B*\text{log}_2(1 + \frac{S}{N})$$

- $C$ ... channel capacity (bits/s) theoretical upper bound of bitrate
- $B$ ... bandwidth (hertz)
- $S/N$ ... [[Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR)]]
