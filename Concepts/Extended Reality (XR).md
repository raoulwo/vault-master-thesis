# Extended Reality (XR)

Extended reality is a general term that encompasses:

- Virtual Reality (VR)
- Augmented Reality (AR)
- Mixed Reality (MR)

AR/VR:

- (1) End-to-end latency **must** be *less than 16ms* to avoid perceptional issues.
