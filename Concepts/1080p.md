# 1080p

- *1080p* is a *resolution* of $1920 \times 1080$ pixels
- *1080p* is also known as *Full High-definition (FHD)*
- *p* stands for [[Progressive Scan|progressive scan]]
- *1080p* is also often referred to as *2K* resolution (~2000 pixels horizontally)
