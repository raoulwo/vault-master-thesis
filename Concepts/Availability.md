# Availability

*Availability* has two meanings:

- The degree to which a system is operable state at the start of a mission
- The probability that a system operates at a given point in time

High availability is usually defined in order of *nines*, e.g.:

- 99.9%
- 99.99%
- 99.999%
- ...
