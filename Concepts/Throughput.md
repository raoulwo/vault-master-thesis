# Throughput

Throughput is the *rate of message delivery* over a communication network. It's usually measured in *bits per second (bit/s or bps)*.

The throughput of communication systems can be affected by various factors:

- Underlying physical medium
- Processing power of system components
- End-user behavior
- ...

When taking *protocol overhead* into account, the useful rate of data transfer can be lower than the maximum throughput. The useful part is called *goodput*.
