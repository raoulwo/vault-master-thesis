# Forward Error Correction (FEC)

*Forward error correction (FEC)* is a technique used to *detect and correct errors* during data transmission through *encoding at the transmitter* and *decoding at the receiver*.

The idea is to encode the data with enough redundant information to recover in case of communication errors. A problem arises when packet loss is so high that not enough of redundant information is successfully received for the data to be recovered.
