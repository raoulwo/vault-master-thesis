# Progressive Scan

*Progressive scanning* (or *non-interlaced scanning*) is a format of displaying video data where *all lines of each frame are drawn in sequence*. This is in contrast to traditional *interlaced scanning*, where all odd rows and then only the even rows are drawn alternately.
