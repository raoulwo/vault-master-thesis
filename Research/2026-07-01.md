## Proposal

Motivation:

- LEO satellites significantly reduce transmission latency compared to GEO satellites
	- Introduces challenges however because non-stationarity -> migrations
- Great use case for VR/AR applications
- VR/AR applications have requirements:
	- Low latency: 10-20ms (one paper says < 16ms)
	- High bitrate: 30-40 Mbps for 1080p resolution
	- Low packet loss: 
		- <1% [[@huang2025]]
		- <= $10^{-5}$ to $10^{-4}$ [[@itu2021]]
- Live migrations
	- Minimizing downtime
	- Minimizing migration time
- Hypothesis
	- Current migration strategies have too high packet loss for VR/AR use case
- Experiments
	- Have to find experiments answering the hypothesis
