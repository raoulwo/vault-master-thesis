Introduction:

- [[Forward Error Correction (FEC)]] can prevent retransmissions
	- Idea: Add redundant repair packets among the packet stream
		- When packet loss occurs, can use repair packets to recover lost packets
	- Streaming FEC codes (convolutional FEC) have elastic encoding windows
	- FEC basically results in a trade-off between increased bandwidth and reduced delay

Motivation:

- Existing packet loss models are insufficient to correctly characterize LEO satellite networks
